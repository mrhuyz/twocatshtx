About Two Cats HTX

At Two Cats HTX, we believe the best memories are made together. Born and raised in Houston, we're on a mission to bring families and communities closer — one claw grab, one birthday party, and one cotton candy cloud at a time. We're not just in the fun business; we're in the people business.

We partner with local businesses and community venues who want to bring something special to their space — offering a simple, low-effort way to earn passive income while giving back to the neighborhoods we love. If you believe in family-first fun and growing together, there's a place for you in the Two Cats family.
